### Lesson: Using "En" and "De" in French

In this lesson, we will cover the usage of the French pronoun "en" and various forms of "de" (du, de la, de l', des). These elements are essential for creating coherent and grammatically correct sentences in French.

#### Keynotes:

1. **Forms of "De":**
   - **du** (de + le): Used with masculine singular nouns.
   - **de la**: Used with feminine singular nouns.
   - **de l'**: Used with singular nouns starting with a vowel.
   - **des** (de + les): Used with plural nouns.

2. **"De" with Locations:**
   - **de + lieu**: Indicates the origin of a movement.
     - Example: venir de (to come from), sortir de (to go out of), rentrer de (to come back from).

3. **Using "En":**
   - "En" replaces phrases introduced by "de."
     - Example: 
       - "Tu bois du café?" → "Oui, j'en bois." / "Non, je n'en bois pas."
       - "Tu fais beaucoup de sport?" → "Oui, j'en fais beaucoup."

4. **Quantity and "En":**
   - When specifying quantities, "en" replaces the phrase that indicates quantity.
     - Example:
       - "Tu achètes deux kilos de tomates?" → "Oui, j'en achète deux kilos."
       - "Tu as un chat?" → "Oui, j'en ai un." / "Je n'en ai pas."

5. **Actions and "En":**
   - When referring to doing something or consuming something, "en" can be used.
     - Example:
       - "Tu aimes faire du judo?" → "Oui, j'aime en faire." / "Je n'aime pas en faire."
       - "Il faut faire du sport?" → "Oui, il faut en faire."

6. **Remembering and "En":**
   - When using verbs like "se souvenir de" (to remember), "en" can replace "de" + object.
     - Example:
       - "Tu te souviens de ton école?" → "Oui, je m'en souviens." / "Je ne m'en souviens pas."

7. **De + Someone (Toniques):**
   - When "de" is followed by a person, use tonic pronouns (moi, toi, lui, elle, nous, vous, eux, elles).
     - Example:
       - "Je me souviens de lui."

8. **Prepositions:**
   - Common prepositions include: chez (at someone's place), pour (for), avec (with), sans (without), à côté de (next to).

9. **Actions and Objects:**
   - Using "en" to replace objects in past actions.
     - Example:
       - "Tu as bu du café?" → "Oui, j'en ai bu." / "Non, je n'en ai pas bu."
       - "Tu as mangé du fromage?" → "Oui, j'en ai mangé." / "Je n'en ai pas mangé."

10. **Verbs and "En":**
    - When referring to the action with a specific object, use "en."
      - Example:
        - "Ton ami a fait de la natation?" → "Oui, il en a fait." / "Non, il n'en a pas fait."
        - "Ta mère a préparé des pâtes?" → "Oui, elle en a préparé." / "Elle n'en a pas préparé."

#### Examples for Practice:
1. **Using "de":**
   - "Tu as des enfants?" → "Oui, j'en ai un." / "Je n'en ai pas."

2. **Quantities:**
   - "Tu achètes deux kilos de pommes?" → "Oui, j'en achète deux kilos."

3. **Memory:**
   - "Tu te souviens de ta maison?" → "Oui, je m'en souviens."

4. **Actions in the Past:**
   - "Tu as mangé du gâteau?" → "Oui, j'en ai mangé." / "Non, je n'en ai pas mangé."

### Practice Exercises:
1. Translate and respond:
   - "Do you drink coffee?" 
     - "Yes, I drink it." / "No, I don't drink it."
   - "Did your friend swim?"
     - "Yes, he swam." / "No, he didn't swim."
   - "Will your parents buy a house?"
     - "Yes, they will buy one." / "No, they won't buy one."
